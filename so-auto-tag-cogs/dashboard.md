## FAQs

**How often are AI Insights updated?**
AI Insights are refreshed each time you visit the Dashboard. They analyze your latest store data in real-time.

**Can I dismiss an insight?**
Some insights can be dismissed temporarily, but they may reappear if the underlying condition persists.

**Why don't I see any insights?**
Insights appear when the system detects patterns in your data. If you have a new store with little data, insights may not be available yet.
