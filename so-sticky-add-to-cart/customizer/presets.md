# Preset Themes

One-click professional themes with coordinated colors, typography, and spacing.

## Quick Apply

1. Open Customizer → **Presets** tab
2. Browse 25+ themes
3. Click **Apply**
4. Customize further (optional)
5. **Save & Deploy**

## How to Apply a Preset

### Steps

1. **Open Customizer**
   - From app dashboard
   - Click "Open Customizer"

2. **Go to Presets Tab**
   - First tab in left panel
   - Shows preset thumbnails

3. **Preview Presets**
   - Click on preset thumbnail
   - See live preview
   - Compare different options

4. **Apply Preset**
   - Click "Apply" button
   - Preset instantly applied
   - All settings updated

5. **Customize Further** (Optional)
   - Tweak colors
   - Adjust fonts
   - Fine-tune spacing

6. **Save & Deploy**
   - Click Save
   - Click Deploy
   - Go live!

## Customizing Presets

### Start with Closest Match

1. Choose preset closest to your brand
2. Apply it
3. Make small adjustments
4. Save custom configuration

### Common Adjustments

**Colors:**
- Change primary to your brand color
- Keep hover as darker shade
- Adjust sale color if needed

**Typography:**
- Keep font or switch to theme font
- Adjust sizes for readability
- Modify weights for emphasis

**Spacing:**
- Match border radius to theme
- Adjust padding for comfort
- Tweak gaps as needed

## Common Questions

**Can I save my own preset?**
Not currently, but you can export code and reapply later.

**Can I switch between presets?**
Yes! Apply different presets anytime. Previous settings are replaced.

**Will it affect my theme?**
No. Presets only affect the sticky cart, not your main theme.

**Can I request a new preset?**
Yes! Contact support with your ideal configuration.
