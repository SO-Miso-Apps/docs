## Multi-Language Stores

### Setup for Multiple Languages

If your store supports multiple languages:

1. **Identify your markets**
   - Which languages do you support?
   - What are the primary customer regions?

2. **Create translations**
   - Use professional translation services
   - Avoid automated translation for customer-facing text
   - Test with native speakers

3. **Configure per language**
   - Shopify supports multi-language themes
   - You may need separate configurations
   - Contact support for multi-language setup

## Character Limits

### Recommended Lengths

**Add to Cart:**
- Maximum: 20 characters
- Optimal: 10-15 characters
- Too long: breaks layout on mobile

**Buy Now:**
- Maximum: 15 characters
- Optimal: 8-12 characters
- Keep concise for button size

**Select Options:**
- Maximum: 25 characters
- Optimal: 15-20 characters
- Shown before variant selection

**Sold Out:**
- Maximum: 20 characters
- Optimal: 8-15 characters
- Should fit in button area

### Testing Length

1. Enter your custom text
2. Check mobile preview
3. Verify text isn't truncated
4. Ensure buttons remain usable

## SEO & Accessibility

### Accessibility Considerations

**Clear language:**
- Use action verbs
- Be descriptive
- Avoid ambiguity
- Consider screen readers

**Examples:**
- Good: "Add to Cart"
- Poor: "Click Here"

### Internationalization Best Practices

1. **Consistent terminology** across your store
2. **Professional translation** for quality
3. **Test with native speakers**
4. **Cultural sensitivity** in wording
5. **Local conventions** (e.g., "Basket" vs "Cart")
